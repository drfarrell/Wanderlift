Wanderlift

Ridesharing for Adventurelovers

Abstract

Wanderlift is a startup founded by two University of Denver students that were frustrated that they couldn't get to the Rocky Mountains without a car. I joined the founding team to catalyze their mission with a complete brand, marketing, and app overhaul. This case will grow with the company, but you can expect to find details of our team's design process here.


"We're in the business of monetizing vacant seats that are already going to a destination."

Visit the Wanderlift Website

This case study is in progress, but you can browse the Wanderlift Website and browse some shots of what I've done so far. You're welcome to download the app, but Wanderlift may not be available in your area. Regardless, the log-in screen alone is worth the download, I promise.
