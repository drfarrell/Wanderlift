Wanderlift

Ridesharing for Adventurelovers

Abstract

Wanderlift is a startup founded by two University of Denver students that were frustrated that they couldn't get to the Rocky Mountains without a car. I joined the founding team to catalyze their mission with a complete brand, marketing, and app overhaul. This case will grow with the company, but you can expect to find details of our team's design process here.


"Collectively, our users have shared over 10,000 miles together in just over 10 weeks"

This case study is in progress, but you can browse the Wanderlift Website as well as some shots of what I've done so far. You're welcome to download the app, but Wanderlift may not be available in your area. Regardless, the log-in screen alone is worth the download, I promise.
